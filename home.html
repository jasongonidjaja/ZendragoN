    Facilities

    Access a symphony of services crafted to indulge your senses and enrich your journey

    Training Grounds

    This facility is where dragons undergo rigorous physical exercises and combat training to hone their combat skills, agility, and strength. Trainers supervise and guide dragons through various drills and simulations to prepare them for real-life challenges and battles.

    Library

    The library is a treasure trove of knowledge, containing ancient texts, scrolls, and tomes on dragon lore, history, magic, and various other subjects. Dragons and their trainers can study here to deepen their understanding of the world, magic, and dragonkind.

    Cultural Center

    The cultural center serves as a hub for dragons to learn about different cultures, traditions, and languages. It provides workshops, performances, and events to promote cross-cultural understanding and appreciation among dragons from diverse backgrounds.

    Laboratories

    In the laboratories, dragons engage in experimental research and scientific exploration under the guidance of scholars and alchemists. They study topics such as magical properties, potion brewing, elemental interactions, and genetic traits to advance dragonkind's knowledge and capabilities.

    Infirmary

    The infirmary is where injured or sick dragons receive medical attention and care from skilled healers and veterinarians. It's equipped with healing spells, potions, and advanced medical equipment to ensure the well-being and swift recovery of injured dragons.

    Garden

    The garden is a tranquil oasis where dragons can relax, meditate, and enjoy nature. It's filled with lush vegetation, enchanted ponds, and soothing landscapes that provide a serene environment for dragons to unwind and rejuvenate after their training and studies.

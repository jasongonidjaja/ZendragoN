    • Isle of Berk

    Explore the origins of ZendragoN Academy on the Isle of Berk, where the timeless bond between humans and dragons first flourished. Delve into the rich history of the academy's inception, as seasoned mentors paved the way for aspiring riders, shaping the future of dragonry. Discover the roots of camaraderie and adventure that laid the foundation for the legendary academy. Welcome to the Isle of Berk, where the saga of ZendragoN begins.

    How It Started

    2023

    The Dawn of a Dream
    Before ZendragoN Academy took flight over Berk, Jason, an explorer, met Kalista, a dragon researcher. They shared a vision: a sanctuary for humans and dragons to learn together, fostering understanding.

    Under Berk's starlit sky, Jason and Kalista conceived the idea for ZendragoN Academy. Fueled by passion and determination, they set out to redefine the human-dragon relationship for generations to come.

    2050

    Forging Bonds Amidst Flames
    In 2050, ZendragoN Academy rose on Berk as a beacon of hope amid challenges. Cultivating human-dragon connections demanded patience, understanding, and unwavering dedication.

    Students and instructors embarked on daring quests, facing dragons and Berk's wilderness to earn their trust. Through trials, bonds formed, transcending barriers. Success laid groundwork for a future of human-dragon equality at ZendragoN.

    2070

    The Evolution of Harmony
    By 2070, ZendragoN Academy was a bastion of harmony. Perseverance and innovation addressed initial challenges, fostering a symbiotic human-dragon relationship of trust and respect.

    Mistrust and conflict between species were replaced by dragons and humans soaring together above Berk. ZendragoN Academy exemplified unity, inspiring future generations to embrace diversity and forge connections across boundaries.

    2119

    A Legacy of Unity
    In 2119, ZendragoN Academy stood tall, symbolizing unity and progress. Laughter and camaraderie filled its halls, a testament to a dream transcending time and space, born from two visionaries.

    ZendragoN Academy today upholds its founders' values, nurturing future dragon riders, scholars, and adventurers. Amidst thriving Berk, it showcases collaboration and understanding's power in overcoming adversity. In a world of division, ZendragoN Academy exemplified unity through common goals. Its legacy endures, flames of unity burning eternally.
